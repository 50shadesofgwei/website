My first project was teaching myself to play the guitar at the age of 12. I have played ever since, and like to think I'm half decent at it.

In 2021 I dropped out of university to start working in MEV and programmatic trading at what is now Special Mechanisms Group.

Later, I started programming trading bots on my own; work for which I was awarded around $70,000 throughout 2023 and early 2024 in grants from organisations such as GMX and Synthetix.

A part of this work surrounding funding rate arbitrage is public and can be found here.

This work later developed into a small private trading operation, for which $1m of investor capital was raised.

In 2025 I was headhunted to join Chaos Labs as a Researcher specialising in on-chain Perpetual Futures.

In March 2026 I founded Pathfinder Quantum Technologies Ltd. The company mission is to tackle scalability of qubit systems not by error correction, but by eliminating the hardware noise which causes error correction to be necessary in the first place.
