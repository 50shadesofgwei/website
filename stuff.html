In 2021 I dropped out of university to start working in MEV and programmatic trading at what is now Special Mechanisms Group. Later, I started programming trading bots on my own; work for which I was awarded around $70,000 throughout 2023 and early 2024 in grants from organisations such as GMX and Synthetix.

A part of this work surrounding funding rate arbitrage is public and can be found here. This work later developed into a small private trading operation, for which $1m of investor capital was raised. In 2025 I was headhunted to join Chaos Labs as a Researcher specialising in on-chain Perpetual Futures.

In March 2026 I founded Pathfinder Quantum Technologies Ltd. in order to design and manufacture photonically integrated trapped ion chips.
