Ruy Lopez, Closed

Summary

Some thoughts about playing weaker players; 1st, 2nd and 3rd is to not get cocky! I knew he was weaker than I was but in order to prove that, I have to play accurately. I have to play the most accurately, I have to be deadly precise, I have to punish all of their minor mistakes. This is the way to win against these players. I cannot assume that I can just play lazily and win anyway.

Position 1

Here I played c3 on instinct without much thought (a common motif in this game!), and missed the fact that Bxc6 dxc6 Nxe5 already works. I remember thinking that castling wasn’t the move here, but did not invest any serious time into thinking about why that was. I don’t think anyone has ever actually played castles against me here, but either way I should have noticed that there’s a remove the defender tactic against e5. Will be cognisant of this theme in future.
